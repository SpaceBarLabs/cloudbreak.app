What is FeedBin Essential?

FeedBin Essential is a modern, fast RSS feed reader that lets you follow your favorite websites and blogs in one place. We host and maintain FeedBin Essential for you, so you don't have to worry about server management or updates.

How much does it cost?

Please visit our main page for current pricing.

10% of profits are donated to the FeedBin project.

What apps do you recommend for reading FeedBin Essential?

FeedBin Essential works with many popular RSS reader apps. For iOS, the official FeedBin app provides the best experience. For Android, we recommend News Aggregator. Any app that supports the FeedBin API should work well with our service.

How long does account creation take?

In most cases, your account will be created quickly after your subscription is processed. However, in some cases, it may take up to two business days to complete the account setup.

What kind of support do you provide?

We focus on providing a reliable, high-uptime FeedBin hosting service. Our support is limited to the information provided in this FAQ. While we maintain and update the FeedBin installation, we do not provide technical support or tutorials for using FeedBin itself - for that, please refer to the official FeedBin documentation.
