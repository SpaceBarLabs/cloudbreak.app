Technical Support

Our Support Policy

To keep our products affordable, we do not offer free, one-on-one technical support. Our community forum is the primary way to get help, and it relies on peer-to-peer (user-to-user) assistance.

Join Our Community Forum

Details

  • The community forum is not an official support channel with guaranteed response times.
  • The user community, not the CloudBreak team, is the first line of support on the community forum.
  • Please be respectful and patient. Everyone on the community forum is helping in their spare time.

Team Engagement & The Tip Jar
