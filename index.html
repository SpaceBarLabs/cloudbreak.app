What is CloudBreak?

CloudBreak is a privacy-focused RSS hosting service that offers ethical alternatives to big tech services like Google Reader and Feedly. We prioritize your data privacy and ownership.

What services does CloudBreak offer?

CloudBreak currently offers FreshRSS and Feedbin Mini hosting services, with more options potentially coming in the future based on user requests.
